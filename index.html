Machine-verified mathematics for frontier models.

Millennium Research turns published mathematics into machine-checked Lean 4 proofs, then structures that verified corpus into an instrument for measuring how models reason. The long-term aim is bigger: AI that can make real progress on mathematics no one has solved.

Where this is going.

Measurement is the foundation, not the destination. The reason to know exactly how strong a model’s reasoning is, is to push it further, toward mathematics that is genuinely hard. Our north star is a system that can make real progress on problems that have resisted human effort for decades, like the open questions in the Erdős collection. You can’t get there without first being able to tell, rigorously, when a machine’s proof is real. That’s what we build.
